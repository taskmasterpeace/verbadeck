# VerbaDeck Use Cases & Target Audiences

## 🎯 Overview

VerbaDeck provides three powerful modes for different use cases:
1. **Create from Scratch** - AI-guided presentation creation
2. **Process Existing Content** - Convert scripts/slides to voice-driven presentations
3. **Know It All Wall** - Voice-activated Q&A practice mode

---

## 📊 Know It All Wall - Primary Use Cases

### 1. 🎤 **Job Interview Preparation**
**Target Users:** Job seekers, career changers, recent graduates

**How It Works:**
- Upload your resume and job description
- AI generates common interview questions
- Practice answering with voice keyword confirmation
- Export your Q&A session as a study guide

**Example Workflow:**
```
1. Upload resume for "Senior Data Scientist" role
2. AI asks: "Tell me about your experience with machine learning"
3. Two answer options appear with keywords
4. Say keywords to confirm your chosen approach
5. Export all answers as interview prep guide
```

**Benefits:**
- ✅ Practice realistic interview scenarios
- ✅ Build confidence with voice responses
- ✅ Get AI-generated answer frameworks
- ✅ Review and refine your responses
