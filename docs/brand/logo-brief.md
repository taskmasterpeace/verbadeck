# VerbaDeck — logo brief

**Mode:** refine (an existing mark exists — `client/public/logo.png` + `logo-icon.png`).

## What it is
VerbaDeck is a voice-driven presentation studio: say a trigger word and the slide advances —
no clicker — and AI answers the room's questions live from your own deck.

## Audience
Founders pitching VCs, sales engineers demoing live, conference speakers — people who present
with their hands full and their reputation on the line. The mark has to look credible in a
boardroom and on a GitHub README, not cute.

## Personality
Confident · precise · modern · voice-forward · premium. **Not** playful, organic, or "wellness."

## Colors (from `docs/brand.json` — no purple, and refine away from green)
- **Primary — ocean blue:** `#0076A5` (bright variant `#15a6d6` for dark backgrounds)
- **Accent — teal:** `#00A9B5` / `#2dd4bf`
- **Ink (dark ground):** `#0a0f14`
- Display type elsewhere in the brand: Sora; mono labels: JetBrains Mono.

## What's wrong with the current mark (the refine targets)
The current icon is a microphone built from a feather/leaf with voice waves, in a
blue→teal→**green** gradient.
- **Too busy** — the feather barbs, sun-burst, and stacked swooshes turn to mush at 16px (favicon).
- **No one-color version** — it leans entirely on a gradient; it can't be stamped in a single ink.
- **Off-brand green** — the palette is ocean-blue + teal; the green reads "nature/wellness app."
- **Wrong genre** — organic/ornate, when the product is a sharp AI tool for founders.

**Keep:** the microphone = voice idea (it's exactly on-message). **Fix:** drop the green, drop the
ornament, simplify to a geometric mark, and add a quiet "advance / forward" cue so the symbol
says *voice → the slide moves*.

## Motif ideas (most important — anchor every prompt here)
1. **A "V" built from a symmetric voice waveform** whose bars taper to a point that doubles as a
   forward/play chevron — monogram (V) + voice + "advance" in one clean thought. *(lead)*
2. **A clean geometric microphone** with a small forward play-chevron in the capsule — keeps
   continuity with today's mark, but flat, single-weight, and 16px-legible.
3. **A stacked "deck"** (two rounded slide cards) nudged forward by a single voice pulse.
4. **A sound wave resolving into a play/forward triangle.**

## Must-work conditions
- One flat color (no gradient) at full strength.
- Legible at **16px** favicon and as a circular/squared app icon.
- Reads on both the light app chrome and the dark presenter/landing backgrounds.
- Pairs cleanly with the "VerbaDeck" wordmark in Sora/Inter.

## Avoid
Green; gradient-as-a-crutch; the feather/leaf/sun ornament; rounded "bubbly" type; and the
AI-logo clichés — globes, light bulbs, gears, generic swooshes, speech-bubble-with-soundwave,
hexagon tech badges, 3D bevels.
