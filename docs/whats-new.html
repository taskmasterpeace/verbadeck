v1.2.0 June 24, 2026

Your private, invite-only studio — host it yourself, behind your own door.

New
  • Invite-only access. A single-owner sign-in gates the whole app; everyone else lands on a waitlist.
  • Run it as your own hosted tool. One command puts the entire app — voice, AI, the Knowledge Brain — behind your own private URL over a Cloudflare tunnel. No port-forwarding; your API keys never leave your machine.
Improved
  • The landing page now leads with the VerbaDeck mark as its brand anchor.
  • A clearer first run — "Create" drops you into the builder; the audience screen and phone remote explain what to do when nothing's live; menus and preset controls fit small phones.
Fixed
  • Microphone trouble now explains itself — blocked, missing, or busy mics get plain-English guidance, and you can always advance with the arrow keys.
  • Errors show as tidy notifications instead of blocking pop-ups.
  • The Knowledge Brain points you to add a document before you ask it a question.
Access
  • Owner-only protection on every API call and the live voice connection, plus a one-line password-recovery command.

v1.1.0 June 22, 2026

Recall your whole knowledge base, live — and a home on the web.

New
  • Knowledge Brain. Paste a large, growing body of text and recall it instantly during live Q&A — it finds the few most relevant passages and answers in about a second. Each document is auto-titled and topic-tagged.
  • A home on the web. A polished landing page — interface tour, feature highlights, and an honest comparison — deploying to verbadeck.com.
  • Drive it from code. Add, search, and ask the Knowledge Brain through a simple programmatic interface and a public endpoint.
Improved
  • Live presenter Q&A now answers from your Knowledge Brain, not just the current slide.

v1.0.0 June 21, 2026

The production-ready 1.0 — present a real deck hands-free, from first run to closing slide.

New
  • Voice-driven presenting. Say a trigger word and the slide advances — no clicker, no keyboard.
  • Live AI Q&A from your own deck, a cinematic audience view, a presenter cockpit, and three ways to build a deck.
Improved
  • A premium design system end to end, a faster initial load, and full accessibility + brand polish.
